About us

Behind this Online Aquarium Store, there is a passionate hobbyist who had shown his interest in aquariums from his young days when he was just 8 years of old. As you might not expect he did not started his commercial venture in aquariums from the start. He completed his diploma in computer science and started a computer consultancy service at the age of 19 and then he joined his family business of printing and publishing.

He converted his childhood passion into a commercial venture only after acquiring a commendable experience in the business world and establishing a good clientele. Aquariums India was started as a commercial venture in 2005 and since he started to import exotic aquarium fishes & accessories from China, Thailand & Singapore. venture.
